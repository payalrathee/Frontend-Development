HANG SON DOONG

 Hang Son Doong(Mountain River Cave) is the largest cave, till known. Located in the heart of Phong Nhake Bang National Park in the Quang Binh province of Vietnam, it is one of the richest sources of biodiversity. The pristine ecosystem it supports takes you back to the Mesozoic era(the era of dinosaurs). Moreover, what is more fascinating about this gigantic and diversified bio-hole is its weather system and the uncommon potential of creating its own clouds. The unrivaled beauty of its spectacular landscapes attracts tourists from all over the world and the versatile treasure of fauna and fauna it supports, tempts the ecologists all around.
