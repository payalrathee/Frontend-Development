TOP 5 FAVOURITE FOODS
  • Kulfi Falooda
  • Dosa
  • Gulab Jamun
  • Dhokla
  • Custard

KULFI   FALOODA

North Indian traditional ice cream made up of pistachio, rose water and saffron flavoured, slowly simmered milk combined with noodles. It is usually garnished with crushed nuts and jelly.

DOSA

Dosa is world's thinnest griddlecake made with an earth batter that is left to ferment and rise for 8-10 hours.Ginger,onions and green chilies are added to enhance the flavors.It is served with sambhar and varios chutneys, such as ginger, tamarind or coconut chutney.

GULAB   JAMUN

Gulab Jamun is the king of Indian Desserts.Small balls of milk-powder dough, deep fried and dunked into a sugar syrup, is what constitutes this delicious sweet-dish of Indian cuisine.

DHOKLA

Vegeterian Indian snack, originated in Gujarat, made of rice and chickpeas. Often garnished with mustard seeds or coriander and served with various chutneys.

CUSTARD

A sweet or savory mixture of milk and eggs that can either be baked or stirred on the stovetop. Stirred custards are softer than baked custards and can be used as a sauce (or the base for ice cream). Custards require slow cooking and gentle heat in order to prevent separation (curdling); stirred custards are generally made in a double boiler and baked custards in a water bath.
